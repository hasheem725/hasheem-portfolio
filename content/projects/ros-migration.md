### The Challenge
Transitioning a massive fleet from legacy Ubuntu 16.04/ROS1 to a modern, secure ROS2 environment.

### The Impact
* **Build Times:** Reduced by **66%** through Docker optimization.
* **Security:** Implemented SROS2 for encrypted communication.
* **Scale:** Successfully deployed to hundreds of robots in production.
